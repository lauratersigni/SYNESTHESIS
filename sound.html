Karlo Calayag
KEY WORDS: STICKY, AFTERTASTE, JUICY
“Eating the grapefruit on the other hand for me was kind of (you can delete whats in these brackets but dude it was fkn nasty) an unsatisfying experience. I did not like the fruit to
say the least. But I guess it still had this feeling of being fresh fruit, and so I opted to make the composition triangle dominant. I then tried to go by the order of the aftertaste, which was strongly bitter which was then followed by hints of sweetness and sour. The sporadic random placement of the shapes are to kind of represent how I was confused by what I was chewing on.”
