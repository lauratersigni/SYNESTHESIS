taste -> sight
After the taste experiment was completed the participant was asked to visually create what they have just tasted. Using a computer and the illustrator file I created beforehand they are asked to follow the steps provided. On the file it says; Thank you for participating in this mini experiment. After participating in tasting the food. Please use this illustrator file to create your own translation of the food. Each shape is defined with a key descriptive word. Depending on your taste buds try to translate what you have tasted using these shapes. Try to think of the beginning bites and taste compared to the last. Could you create a narrative of your experience with these shapes? Please see the example below for reference. Beside this description they are provided with the shapes they are allowed to use along with the descriptive words to define them. They are told they may change the size of the shapes, and opacity of them.
Sweet       Sour          Fresh           Salty          Bitter       Texture
